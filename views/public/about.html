About

Ecopia Plc is a For Profit Social Organisation headquartered in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Ecopia was founded in 2006 by Dr. Mitslal Kifleyesus-Matschie and Tigist Mengistu. Ecopia stands for 'Ecological Products of Ethiopia' and for high-quality natural foods and cosmetics that are produced using sustainable methods in a socially and environmentally friendly manner.
